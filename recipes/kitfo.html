Kitfo

Kitfo is one of Ethiopian traditional delicious food, that has lots of ingredients in it. This traditional food is mainly found around the northern part of Ethiopia and around the capital.

But one thing to remind, Kitfo should be prepared and cooked by a very handy woman to enjoy it well.

Enjoy your Kitfo!

Ingredients

  • average of 3 to 6 kg of smoothly grinded onion
  • pepper
  • butter
  • oil
  • meat
  • about 4 to 5 spice and relish
  • salt and others as the specific chief need
  • Kocho
