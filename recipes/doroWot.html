Doro Wot

Doro Wot is one of Ethiopian traditional delicious food, that has lots of ingredients in it. Mainly it contains an average of 3 to 6 kg of smoothly grinded onion, pepper, butter,
oil, chicken meat, about 4 or 5 spice and relish, obviously salt and others. But one thing to remind, Doro Wot should be prepared and cooked by a very handy woman to enjoy it well.

Enjoy your Doro!
