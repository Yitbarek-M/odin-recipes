Firfir

Firfir is one of Ethiopian traditional delicious food, that has lots of ingredients in it. This traditional food is mainly found around the northern part of Ethiopia and around the capital.

But one thing to remind, Doro Wot should be prepared and cooked by a very handy woman to enjoy it well.

Enjoy your Doro!

Ingredients

  • average of 3 to 6 kg of smoothly grinded onion
  • pepper
  • butter
  • oil
  • chicken meat
  • about 4 to 5 spice and relish
  • salt and others as the specific chief need

Steps

  1. First, the onion should be chopped off tiddly
  2. Then the onion should be roasted like up to 2 hours only with water, since the main part of it is this
  3. Then again roast it with oil for about 30 mins
  4. Then add enough pepper and roast it yet again
  5. Ever since the chief feels like the roasting step is over, add the meat and the bone
  6. Fill enough water after about 30 mins the meat is added
  7. Finally, add butter and other spices
