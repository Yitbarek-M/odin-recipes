Firfir

Firfir is one of Ethiopian traditional delicious food, that has lots of ingredients in it. This traditional food is mainly found around the northern part of Ethiopia and around the capital.

But one thing to remind, Firfir should be prepared and cooked by a very handy woman to enjoy it well.

Enjoy your Firfir!

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • pepper
  • butter
  • oil
  • meat
  • Injera, Ethiopian historical food
  • salt and others chief's specific ingredients
